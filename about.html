About

I’m Sarah, the face behind the flowers. Based in the lovely village of Bourn in South Cambridge, my studio based floral business provides bespoke seasonal designs for weddings, events and special occasions throughout Cambridgeshire and surrounding areas.


I have received training from the wonderful Cambridge flower school and continue to educate myself as we navigate towards a more sustainable industry. I am led by the seasons when selecting only the best individual flowers, using British and locally grown blooms wherever possible, and providing alternatives to the use of floral foam, which in recent years has been found to be so damaging to the environment.

My meticulous attention to detail coupled with my creativity and passion for all things floral will ensure that whatever I’m creating will be unique and beautifully arranged. I always take into great consideration the person or couple that I am creating for, ensuring that the flowers reflect and represent them.

So rest assured that regardless of the size of your wedding, be it just bouquets and button holes, or a much larger affair, whether flowers are immensely important to you or if they are simply a box that needs ticking off your list! Your flowers will be the most important thing to me, and I will pour my heart into arranging each and every stem.
